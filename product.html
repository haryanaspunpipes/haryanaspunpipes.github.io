Concrete Manhole Covers

We offer our customers superior quality Double Main Whole Cover, which are designed and developed using advance technology and machinery. The offered covers are used on main roads for protection against accidents in pit holes. These main hole covers are manufactured in accordance with the defined quality standards. Known for features like resistant to heat & abrasion, longer service life and optimum performance, these are offered to our customers at industry leading prices.

Kerb Stone (Curb Stone)

We are one of the renowned manufacturers of Kerb Stones which are widely used in making garden and footpath fencing and for other construction purposes. These stones are used for enhancing the appearance of the footpaths, pavements and gardens. To facilitate our valued customers, we also offer customization facility.

Interlocking Tiles

Our precast concrete decorative interlocking pavers have wide application as exterior surface covers. The heavy duty pavers are used in city streets, loading ramps, intersections, gas stations, loading docks, industrial floors, etc. The medium ones are used in sidewalks, shopping malls, canal lining, residential streets, safety zones, parks, storage yards, camping areas, etc. The light varieties have application driveways, pool decks, bicycle paths, walk ways, temporary paving, and more.

DRAIN COVER

We are one of the leading manufacturers of PVC Moulds for Drain Covers. These are fabricated in accordance with the latest industrial patterns. Our moulds are available in 300 mm x 600 mm x 60 mm size in rectangular shapes and are used for the purpose of covering drains and prevent the risk of accidents.

Three Box Paver Block

Our dreamland blocks or concrete paver block (Three Box) can be laid without cement bonding so that we can remove and relay to attend below the ground work. These concrete paver block are available in a range of five vibrant colors such as terracotta, saffron, dholphur, olive green and dove grey. Easy to lay and available in endless combinations, these blocks are ideal for ideal for heavy-duty floors with an excessive load bearing strength enhancing elegance to driveways, sit-out floors, landscaping, car parking, walkways and pathways.

R.C.C Hume Pipes

The RCC pipes are cast by compacting concrete together with the embedded steel reinforcement in moulds with centrifugal force by revolving the molds using a motor or an engine. These pipes (Also known as Spun Pipes) are used for sewerage, drainage, culverts,irrigation,water supply transportation,service lines etc.

Jacking Pipe

Is a method of laying underground pipelines by assembling the pipes at the foot of an access shaft and pushing them through the ground .
